The Biggest Fishing Festival in Puerto Rico

More Than a Tournament

The International Fishing Festival is Puerto Rico's premier celebration of sport fishing culture. Set against the stunning coastline of Dorado, the festival brings together anglers, families, and the local community for two unforgettable days. Enjoy live music from top Caribbean artists, savor dishes from the island's best food vendors, browse handcrafted goods from local artisans, and cheer on anglers in competitive fishing weigh-ins. Whether you're a seasoned fisherman or here for the party, there's something for everyone.

Festival Venue

Where It Happens

Club Deportivo
Villa Pesquera

Nestled on the beautiful coast of Dorado, Puerto Rico, Club Deportivo at Villa Pesquera is the perfect beachside venue for the International Fishing Festival. With ample parking, waterfront views, and easy access from San Juan (just 30 minutes west on PR-22), the venue offers an unforgettable setting for two days of fishing, food, and celebration.
