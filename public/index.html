Our Story

Born from a love of the sea.

The International Fishing Team started with a simple belief: the waters of Puerto Rico deserve a world-class sport fishing organization. One that competes at the highest level while staying rooted in the culture and community of the Caribbean.

From our first tournament off the coast of Fajardo to a growing calendar of twelve annual events, IFT has become the home for serious anglers and passionate newcomers alike. We run tournaments, organize all-inclusive trips, and host the biggest fishing festival on the island.

This isn't just about catching fish. It's about building a community, growing the sport, and sharing the magic of Puerto Rico's deep blue waters with the world.
